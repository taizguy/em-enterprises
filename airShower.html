AIR SHOWER

Application

Air Showers are self-contained chambers installed at entrances to cleanrooms and other controlled environments. They minimize particulate matter entering or exiting the clean space. Personnel and materials entering or exiting the controlled environment are “scrubbed” by high velocity HEPA-filtered air jets with velocities of 20-22m/s. Contaminated air is then drawn through the base within the unit, filtered and recirculated. Air showers are various in showering, arranging from single-side, double-side, or top-side or combined, and have different door types, manually-operated door, auto-door and shutter door

Main Characters

  1. Pass box is made of SUS 304 stainless steel or power-coated steel plate.
  2. Top air supply with H14 HEPA Filter (99.995%~99.999%@0.3um), reach up to ISO-5 (Class 100)
  3. Double doors are Interlocking to prevent from cross-contamination
  4. External flush surface and ace corner ensure no dust collection and easy cleaning
  5. Dedicated sanitary rubber strip to ensure airtightness
  6. Equipped with UV light
  7. Safe structural design, easy installation
